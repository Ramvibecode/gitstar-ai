# ERP CEO Brief Loop

## Purpose
Generate a concise AP/AR, PO, logistics, and risk summary for leadership review.

## Steps
1. Read ERP state.
2. Identify overdue AP and AR.
3. Identify shipment risks.
4. Identify missing documents or telex issues.
5. Draft CEO summary.
6. Draft recommended actions.
7. Wait for approval before sending.

## Safety
- Verify figures before sending.
- Do not email automatically in L1/L2.
- Keep audit trail.
