# Content Factory Loop

## Purpose
Turn content ideas into publish-ready drafts with scripts, visuals, captions, and thumbnails.

## Steps
1. Select niche and audience.
2. Generate 10 ideas.
3. Pick top idea.
4. Draft script.
5. Draft visual prompts.
6. Draft voiceover.
7. Draft title, description, and hashtags.
8. Queue for human approval.

## Safety
- Original content only.
- No copyrighted characters.
- No misleading synthetic identity claims.
- Human approval before publishing.
